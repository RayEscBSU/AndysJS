About Us

Andy's is a professional auto repair shop located at 321 Happy Day Blvd. in Caldwell. We specialize in repair and maintenance services for General Motors (GM), Ford, and other domestic vehicles. Our primary area of expertise lies in transmissions, offering honest and skillful work for all transmission-related issues.

At our facility, we pride ourselves on providing top-quality service and are certified by ACDelco, ASE, and O'Reilly. We are also an authorized retailer for ACDelco products, displaying our commitment to using high-quality parts for repairs.

We understand the value of customer satisfaction and strive to exceed expectations. As part of our dedication to convenient service, we honor extended warranties, ensuring that our customers' investments are protected. Our knowledgeable team members are fluent in both English and Spanish, ensuring clear communication with our diverse clientele.

Whether you need a simple repair or extensive maintenance, visit our shop or call us at 208-453-1968. Our skilled technicians are ready to provide exceptional service for all your automotive needs.
